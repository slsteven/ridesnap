Step 1

Give us the basics.

We start by getting basic information about your car, like its make, model and year so we can give you an idea of a potential listing price compared to a dealership.

 

Step 2

make an appointment.

Schedule and appointment at any location and we will come to you. We want to meet to do a few things in person:

1. Inspect your car and discuss a competitive listing price based on your needs.
2. Collect the car title and additional paperwork so we can have it prepared for prospective buyers.
3. Give you a BUY IT NOW price
 

Step 3

sit back and relax.

Continue using your car if you need to. We take care of everything from here. We clean your car, photograph it, inspect, post ads, and schedule test drives. We want to manage the complete process so you don't lift a finger.

 

Step 4

Collect your money.

Once your car is sold, we will write you a check. We guarantee that your car will sell in 31 days or we give you the option of having us purchase from you for the BUY IT NOW price. You make money no matter what.
